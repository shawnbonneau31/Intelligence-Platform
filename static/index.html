Carrier Mandate Timeline

Mercury Insurance

Effective: January 2024 | Status: Active | States: CA, AZ, NV, TX

Requires whole-home automatic shutoff devices for new policies on homes over $750K. Premium discount of 5-12% for compliant homes. Piloting expansion to all new construction.

Chubb

Effective: March 2024 | Status: Active | States: All US

Mandatory leak detection for high-value homes ($2M+). Partnered with three IoT providers. Claims data shows 40% reduction in water damage severity for equipped homes.

Farmers Insurance

Effective: July 2024 | Status: Active | States: FL, TX, CA

Incentive program: 8% premium reduction for whole-home water monitoring. Moving to mandate for coastal FL properties by Q1 2026.

Nationwide

Effective: Q2 2026 (Projected) | Status: Pending | States: OH, PA, VA, NC

Draft policy requires water mitigation devices for homes with prior water claims. Currently in underwriting committee review. Expected to cover 2.1M policies.

State Farm

Effective: 2026 (Projected) | Status: Pilot | States: FL, IL

Pilot program in Florida and Illinois testing premium incentives for smart water devices. Early results show 35% claim frequency reduction. Nationwide rollout expected.

Munich Re (Reinsurance)

Effective: 2025 | Status: Active (Reinsurance) | Scope: Global

Offering reinsurance premium credits for cedants that mandate water mitigation technology. Driving downstream carrier adoption. Published white paper on IoT-enabled water risk reduction.
